Xiaohao Yang Resume Publication Work About

Hi, I'm Xiaohao Yang, a PhD student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I hold two master's degrees in Landscape Architecture and Spatial Data Science at Umich. I work as a research assistant in South China Urban Lab (SCULAB) and School for Environment And Sustainability (SEAS) of Umich.

As a researcher, my interest focuses on fusing data science and extended reality to visualize and analyze the built environment and contribute to environmental sustainability and public engagement in the process of planning and design. As a generative designer/coder, I am exploring visual/spatial art using code.
