Hi, I'm Xiaohao Yang. I am an incoming PhD student at the University of Michigan. I am a dual-degree master's student in Landscape Architecture and Spatial Data Science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I work as a research assistant in South China Urban Lab (SCULAB) and School for Environment And Sustainability (SEAS) of Umich. My current interest focuses on fusing data science and extended reality to visualize and analyze the built environment and contribute to environmental sustainability and public engagement in the process of planning and design.
