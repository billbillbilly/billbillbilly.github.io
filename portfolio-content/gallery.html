Examples

The gallery displayed the interesting results of ReactiveCanvas. For the same pattern, there is set of parameters can be adjusted to change the results. Based on the control of density, height, and pattern, ReactiveCanvas shows the diversity of generative art. As a new kind of art form, ReactiveCanvas tries to involve computation and hands-on experience in the generating process of artworks.


The 3d object - the matrix of squares can be manipulated by dragged with your mouse. You are also albe to zoom in or out by scolling the wheel. On the control panel, You can adjust the size - the width and heiht - of the matrix. In other word, you will get more columns or rows. To change the density of matrix, you can set interval - the gap between each squares and planSize - the side length of each squares.
