Gallery

The gallery displayed the interesting results of ReactiveCanvas. For the same pattern, there is set of parameters can be adjusted to change the results. Based on the control of density, height, and pattern, ReactiveCanvas shows the diversity of generative art. As a new kind of art form, ReactiveCanvas tries to involve computation and hands-on experience in the generating process of artworks.
